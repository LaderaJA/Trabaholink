PhilSys Automated Verification

For PhilSys IDs, we offer automated verification through the official PhilSys verification website. This provides faster verification and a verified badge on your profile.

How does PhilSys verification work?
  1. We extract the QR code from your uploaded PhilSys ID image
  2. The QR data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  3. Our system verifies the QR code through the official PhilSys website
  4. Verification results are stored securely (only masked data is visible)
  5. Upon success, you receive a "PhilSys Verified" badge on your profile

Privacy: We only store encrypted QR data and masked PhilSys Card Number (e.g., 1234-****-****-5678). All data is automatically deleted after 30 days.
